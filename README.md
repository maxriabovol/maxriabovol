# Junior Business Analyst
## Professional Profile
Junior Business Analyst with comprehensive training in business analysis, requirements engineering, and the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC). Skilled in eliciting, analyzing, documenting, and managing business requirements, creating user stories and use cases, and translating business needs into clear, actionable requirements and solutions. Strong communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills with the ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders and cross-functional teams in Agile environments.
## Key Skills
* **Requirements:** Requirements Elicitation, Requirements Analysis, Requirements Documentation, User Stories (INVEST), Use Cases, and Change Management.
*  **Technical:** SQL, SDLC, Agile (Scrum, Kanban).
* **Problem Solving:** Root Cause Analysis, Solution Design, Risk Assessment, Process Improvement.
*  **Business Development:** Market Analysis, Client Acquisition, Sales Strategy, Partnership Building, Business Growth, Opportunity Identification.
* **Customer Service:** Client Communication, Customer Support, Conflict Resolution, Complaint Management, Customer Satisfaction.
